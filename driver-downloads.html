Before You Update

Driver updates are low-risk when done cleanly, but avoid stacking random utilities or overlapping RGB packages.

  • Confirm the exact GPU, CPU platform, and motherboard before installing anything.
  • Update chipset and networking packages before troubleshooting graphics or USB issues.
  • Only use one hardware monitoring or RGB control suite when possible.
  • If stability matters more than newest features, ask FORGE which known-good version was validated on your build.
