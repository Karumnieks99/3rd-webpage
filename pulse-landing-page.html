What warranty is included?

Every system includes 3-year parts coverage plus direct builder support for setup questions, diagnostics, and upgrade planning.

Can I request a specific look or theme?

Yes. We can tune the case, lighting intensity, accent colors, cooling approach, and internal presentation around a specific visual direction.

Do you help choose parts for streaming or editing?

Yes. Share your games, software, workflow, and budget and we will balance the build around those actual tasks instead of a generic gaming-only preset.

How are systems packaged for shipping?

Systems are braced internally, double-boxed, and packed to reduce movement and shipping stress on heavier components and glass panels.
